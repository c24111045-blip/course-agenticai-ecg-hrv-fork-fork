Project Codename: The Burnout Shield
—— "Preventative Maintenance" for Human Capital & Automated Risk Hedging
1. Core Concept Reimagined: Not Insurance, but "Preventative Maintenance"
In the Industry 4.0 era, factories install sensors on expensive machinery. If a sensor detects "overheating" or "abnormal vibration," the system automatically pauses the machine or injects coolant to prevent a catastrophic breakdown.
"The Burnout Shield" applies this exact logic to a company’s most expensive asset—its Employees.
•	Traditional Model (Reactive Repair): Employee burns out  Takes long-term sick leave  Company pays high medical costs & loses productivity (Cost > $5,000 USD).
•	Our Model (Preventative Maintenance): System detects physiological stress $ Immediate Micro-Intervention  System provides a coffee or a half-day off (Cost < $50 USD).
We are not paying out based on "feelings"; we are leveraging Agentic AI to execute a low-cost stop-loss mechanism.
 
2. The Mechanism: Leveraging ECG/PPG Data
We utilize ECG (Electrocardiogram) and PPG (Photoplethysmogram) data collected from wearable devices to transform subjective "fatigue" into objective "maintenance signals."
Step 1: The Sensor Trigger
•	Scenario: An employee feels overwhelmed after a high-pressure meeting and records a 1-minute ECG/PPG snapshot using a wearable.
•	Data Nature: This is not just a health checkup; this is a "diagnostic data log" of the human engine.
Step 2: The Agentic Diagnostic (Multi-Agent System)
Once the data is uploaded, our Multi-Agent System takes over immediately, requiring zero human intervention:
1.	Forensic Agent (The Digital Technician):
o	Role: Analyzes the raw CSV waveforms.
o	Action: It calculates RMSSD (a Heart Rate Variability metric). Think of this as measuring engine temperature. If RMSSD is too low (e.g., < 20ms), the parasympathetic nervous system is suppressed—the body is "overheating."
o	Integrity: It simultaneously checks waveform characteristics to filter out fraud or noise (e.g., elevated heart rate due to exercise vs. stress).
2.	Actuary Agent (The Threshold Controller):
o	Role: Cross-references the data with the "Maintenance Contract" (Policy).
o	Action: Verifies if the extracted metrics trigger the intervention clause (e.g., "Trigger payout if critical stress markers appear in two consecutive readings").
3.	Executor Agent (The Cooler):
o	Role: Executes the "Cooling Procedure."
o	Action: Triggers an API to instantly send a $5 Coffee Voucher or automatically approve a Paid Half-Day Off.
 
3. Why Agentic AI? (Solving the Cost Paradox)
Historically, this business model was impossible because the "Administrative Cost" was higher than the "Payout Cost."
•	The Pain Point: Hiring a human underwriter to review an ECG costs ~$30 in labor, but the payout is only a $5 coffee. Commercially, this is suicide.
•	The Enabler: Agentic AI makes this viable.
o	Marginal Cost Near Zero: AI processes the CSV data and executes the transfer for less than $0.10.
o	Real-Time Reaction: The time from data upload to voucher receipt is under 30 seconds. This is the "Golden Hour" for rescue before a breakdown occurs.
